Why SEPPmail matters in messaging and security environments

Automated encryption

SEPPmail describes automated email encryption and decryption without user interaction and support for S/MIME, OpenPGP, TLS and SSL.

High availability and performance

SEPPmail highlights multi-master clustering, active load balancing and high availability for enterprise environments.

Integration and operations

LDAP/AD integration, monitoring, reporting and adaptable security policies create strong touchpoints for delivery and operations.
