## In short

This repository is not just a technical collection of ARM, networking and operations artifacts. It is also structured to work as a **sales and presales asset** for SEPPmail-related projects through **Synedat Group GmbH**.

The repository is built around three key questions:

1. How can **SEPPmail-related high-availability and cluster scenarios on Azure** be prepared in a structured way?
2. How can that become a **traceable, secure and governance-ready delivery approach**?
3. Why is **Synedat** a strong counterpart for architecture, implementation, automation and operational handover?

## Why this repo is now better suited for sales and presales

The repository was already strong for purely technical audiences. For use as a sales medium through Synedat, it previously lacked a few things:

- a clear **hero message with customer value**
- visible **positioning of Synedat as the contact point**
- a direct connection to **SEPPmail value areas** such as encryption, high availability, integration and compliance
- a **CTA structure** for demos, architecture discussions and project inquiries

These points are now emphasized more clearly in the current version.

## Why SEPPmail

According to SEPPmail, the Secure E-Mail Gateway supports automated email encryption, common standards such as S/MIME, OpenPGP, TLS and SSL, GINA for spontaneous encryption, multi-master clustering, active load balancing, LDAP/AD integration, and monitoring via SNMP and syslog. On its partner and support pages, SEPPmail also states that customers are supported by certified or accredited partners during evaluation, introduction and ongoing operation. See `docs/SEPPMAIL-REFERENCES.md`.

## Why through Synedat

Synedat Group GmbH positions this repository as a public working foundation for **SEPPmail-related projects with a focus on architecture, Azure operations, automation, documentation and controlled introduction**.

For customer conversations, Synedat should not appear only as a maintainer, but as a clear point of contact for:

- architecture and onboarding workshops
